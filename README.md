## 👋 About Me

Hi, I'm **Harsha**, a Computer Science undergraduate (**First Class**) at the **University of Ruhuna** and an **Undergraduate Trainee Software Engineer at IFS**.

I enjoy building **end-to-end systems** from clean, reactive backends to modern web frontends, and I'm especially drawn to **event-driven microservices, clean architecture, and applied AI / LLM integration**.

Right now I'm building my **CSE AI Investment Intelligence Platform**, an event-driven microservices system that turns Colombo Stock Exchange market data into AI-assisted investment insights. It started as personal curiosity about investing and became complete end-to-end project so far.

Outside of code, I explore new tech, dig into system design, and turn side-project ideas into real, usable products.

📫 **Let's connect**, always open to interesting problems and collaboration.
